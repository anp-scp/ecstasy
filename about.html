Ecstasy is the annual festival of Central Institute of Technology, Kokrajhar. It was started in 2009 as CIT Annual Week and later renamed as "Ecstasy", which means JOY, HAPPINESS, THRILL, DELIGHT. Since then it has become one of the biggest college level festival in Assam. Ecstasy is conducted towards the end of February every year. Held for three days. It comprises of many competition ranging from sports to literacy, cultural events and many more. The greatest attraction being the cultural extravaganza that continues for three dazzling evenings. The cultural evenings of the Ecstasy is an outstanding amalgamation of talents, both local and global. So welcome everyone, to this fantabulous celebration of sportsmanship, cultural prosperity and above all of the celebration of humanity.
