Naalayak The Band

Naalayak is a 4 piece indie rock band from chandigarh. Known for their explosive live acts and pumping music. They are now considered to be the genre defining new era of indie rock.

Nikhil D'Souza

Nikhil D'Souza  is a singer, songwriter and guitarist from Mumbai, India,and is now signed to East West Records of Warner Music UK.Nikhil’s genre and musical style can be loosely defined as eclectic acoustic guitar-based pop. His songs have a full melodic quality to them due to his use of alternative tunings (favoured by such artists as Nick Drake and Jeff Buckley). Nikhil's major musical influences are Sting and Jeff Buckley.
